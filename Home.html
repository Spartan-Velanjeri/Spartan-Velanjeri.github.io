About Me 

I have always been fascinated by tech and how it works since a very young age. Working with Tech allowed me to freeze time (figuratively!)

Fast Forward 2 Years, I began building tech solutions with a focus towards market needs, at PES University, Bengaluru, where I completed my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering (2016-2020) with a 8.43 CGPA

I worked for 1.5 years as a Robotics Research Associate at Centre for Robotics, Automation and Intelligent Systems, PES University and published two papers on Supernumerary Robotic Limbs for Disaster Relief and for the Visually Challenged. I also have a patent pending related to Swarm Drone and Payload capability during my research.
